🜁 What Is Astrocartography?

Astrocartography (also called astrolocality) maps where planetary energies from your natal chart become most potent on Earth’s surface. Each planet has multiple “lines” that trace where it was conjunct key angles at the time of your birth:

  • AC (Ascendant Line) — Where a planet was rising in the east (identity, visibility, activation).

  • DC (Descendant Line) — Where a planet was setting (relationships, reflection, partnerships).

  • MC (Midheaven Line) — Where a planet was at its highest point (career, calling, public life).

  • IC (Imum Coeli Line) — Where a planet was at the lowest point (home, roots, deep healing).

When you travel to or connect with a location on your planetary line, the essence of that planet is amplified and embodied.
