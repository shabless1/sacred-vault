In the Know — Build Your Game
Volume
The amount of an asset changing hands over a given period. Volume is how you confirm whether a price move is real or a head fake. When price moves UP on high volume — that's buyers in control. When price moves up on LOW volume — it may not hold.
Candlestick Chart
Each candle shows four data points: the open, high, low, and close of that time period. A green candle means price closed higher than it opened — buyers won that period. A red candle means sellers won. The body size tells you conviction. The wicks tell you how far price traveled and got rejected.
Moving Average
The average price of an asset over a set number of periods — 50 days, 200 days, etc. It smooths out the noise and shows you the actual trend. When price is ABOVE the 50-day moving average and that MA is rising — that's a bullish structure.
Gold-Silver Ratio
How many ounces of silver it takes to buy one ounce of gold. Currently at 59. When this ratio narrows (silver outperforms gold), it historically signals that silver is waking up and the broader commodity bull market is healthy.
