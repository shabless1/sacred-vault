In the Know — Build Your Game
Bid-Ask Spread
The gap between what a buyer will pay and what a seller will accept. In volatile environments — like a Scorpio full moon week with a Fed transition — spreads widen. Wider spreads mean higher cost to enter and exit positions. The spread is the market telling you how confident or fearful it is.
Market Cap
The total dollar value of all outstanding shares of a company or all coins of a crypto asset. Market cap tells you the SIZE of what you're dealing with — a $77K Bitcoin with a $1.5 trillion market cap moves very differently than a $0.33 TRX with a $28 billion market cap.
FOMC
Federal Open Market Committee — the body within the Federal Reserve that sets interest rate policy. They meet 8 times per year. This Wednesday April 29 is Powell's last FOMC as Fed Chair. Every word he says becomes part of the historical record of how the Fed navigated one of the most volatile years in decades.
Regulatory Clarity
The government has defined the rules for how an asset or industry can operate legally. Without it, institutions cannot deploy capital into crypto at scale. The CLARITY Act gives the US crypto market regulatory clarity. When it passes, institutional capital that has been sitting on the sideline has a legal lane to enter. That capital movement is what changes price structure — not speculation.
